FAQ

Frequently asked

Do I need my own email domains?

Yes, you will need to provide your own domains. We recommend purchasing secondary domains specifically for outreach to protect your main company domain. Our platform easily handles the integration via standard SMTP/IMAP.

How does the AI personalization work?

Our engine integrates directly with leading LLMs. You provide a prompt framework, and we dynamically scrape data from your lead's website/LinkedIn to generate hyper-personalized intro lines and context for every single email sent.

Is inbox warm-up included?

Absolutely. Every connected inbox automatically enters our peer-to-peer warmup network. We simulate human-like conversations, mark emails as important, and remove them from spam to ensure maximum deliverability.

Can I integrate with my CRM?

Yes! OutreachX connects seamlessly with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and virtually any other CRM through our native Zapier app or directly via our REST API and Webhooks.
